**Recommended pick: #1 — "How ChatGPT & Claude Actually Work (System Design)"**
It matches the searched phrase, front-loads two huge keywords, and the parenthetical "(System Design)" both qualifies the depth and filters for the audience that converts. "Actually" adds a low-cost curiosity nudge without over-promising.

**A/B test:** #1 vs #2 — a clean explainer framing against a punchier "I reverse-engineered" curiosity framing. Same audience, very different click impulse, clean signal.

## 2. Thumbnail Concepts

### Concept 1 — "ENTER → ???" (ranked #1, highest stopping power)
- **Layout**: Full-bleed dark background, a single oversized keyboard ENTER key on the left, a tangled arrow pointing into a glowing black box on the right.
- **Text on image**: **"WHAT HAPPENS?"**
- **Subject**: The Enter key + a mysterious glowing "system" box (no face needed).
- **Color**: Near-black background (#0B0B0F), electric cyan accent on the arrow/glow, white text.
- **Visual hook**: The glowing unknown box — the eye wants to know what's inside.
- **Why it works**: Triggers the curiosity gap from a relatable action (hitting Enter) without lying.

### Concept 2 — Logo Split (ranked #2)
- **Layout**: Hard vertical split — ChatGPT side (left) vs Claude side (right), faint architecture diagram lines behind both.
- **Text on image**: **"INSIDE THE MACHINE"**
- **Subject**: Both logos large, with a simplified flow diagram (boxes + arrows) ghosted in the background.
- **Color**: ChatGPT teal/green left, Claude warm orange right, white center divider with the text.
- **Visual hook**: Two famous logos side by side — instant recognition + comparison pull.
- **Why it works**: Brand recognition does the stopping; the split implies "you'll understand both."

### Concept 3 — Architecture Reveal (ranked #3)
- **Layout**: Clean system-design diagram (user → API → model → tokens) as the hero, one node circled in red.
- **Text on image**: **"DECODED"**
- **Subject**: A real, simplified architecture flowchart.
- **Color**: White/blueprint-blue diagram on dark navy, single red circle as the accent.
- **Visual hook**: The red-circled node — feels like a secret being pointed out.
- **Why it works**: Signals genuine engineering depth; converts the system-design search intent directly.

### Concept 4 — Face + Box (ranked #4)
- **Layout**: Creator face on the right, surprised/curious expression, looking toward a glowing AI brain-box on the left.
- **Text on image**: **"HOW IT REALLY WORKS"**
- **Subject**: Face (strong expression) + glowing neural/brain icon.
- **Color**: Warm-lit face, cool cyan glow on the box for contrast, yellow keyword "REALLY".
- **Visual hook**: Human expression — eyes lock onto a reacting face.
- **Why it works**: Face + curiosity reaction lifts CTR if the channel uses talking-head shots.

**Recommended pairing:** Title #1 + Concept 1. The thumbnail asks the question ("WHAT HAPPENS?") and the title answers the category ("How ChatGPT & Claude Actually Work"). They complete each other instead of repeating, and both stay readable at 320×180.
